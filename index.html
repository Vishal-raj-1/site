    About us

    CARL (Cognitive Applications Research Lab) is an independent student-run Research and Development Lab, started in SRM IST. CARL offers one a platform to enhance and nurture one's research acumen and development abilities.
    By bridging the gap between students and academia, we not only make research easily accessible for everyone but also encourage students to write their own papers.

    WeCare.ai

    Devangi Purkayastha et al.

    "Serving through AI, One Step at a Time" Developed a contextual virtual assistant integrated with the main website to answer any query regarding breast cancer along with a state-of-the-art CNN architecture for Invasive Ductal Carcinoma identification by uploading the histopathology image.

    Document Summarizer, NER and URL Checker

    Devangi Purkayastha

    A web app deployed on Streamlit, and made using Gensim, SpaCy and Sumy to summarize long text documents, analyze docs for named entities as well as check URLs.
